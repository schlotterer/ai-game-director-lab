# Game Bible

## Game Title

Untitled Starter Game

## Child Creative Director

Placeholder only. Do not add real child information unless the family chooses to.

## Big Idea

Blank on purpose. The child should decide what this game is about.

## Main Character

A simple placeholder square. The child should decide who or what it becomes.

## World

A clean placeholder play area. The child should decide the world.

## Core Game Loop

Move, collect an item, increase the score, and chase the next item.

## Controls

Arrow keys move the player.

## Current Features

- Visible play area.
- Placeholder player character.
- Arrow key movement.
- Player stays inside the play area.
- One placeholder item.
- Score increases when the item is collected.
- Item respawns in a random place.
- Win message after 5 items.
- Restart button.
- Light PWA scaffolding.

## Rules of the Game

- Collect 5 items to win.
- The player cannot leave the play area.
- The item moves after each collection.

## Things That Should Stay the Same

- Keep the game web-first.
- Keep it simple enough for a family to inspect.
- Build one approved feature at a time.
- Keep the starter creatively blank until the child gives direction.

## Accessibility / Comfort Notes

- Avoid flashing effects.
- Keep movement simple.
- Keep text short and readable.
- Avoid sudden audio unless adult-approved.
- Avoid forced timers unless the child likes timers.
- Keep visual clutter low.
- Let the child pause, stop, or choose by pointing/letter.
